Family Goals Community

Are you a married man or woman, desiring to be in a community of married men and women, who are united by Agape Love and motivated by a strong desire for mutual learning and modelling their marriages after the original script of God's Word?

Family Goals community, comprising of Ephesians 525 Men and Proverbs 31 women may be the perfect place for you. The E525 Men and P31 Women fold are accountability group and growth groups for people with like minds, seeking to help one another grow and fulfil their family mandates. For more enquiries, kindly send an email to familygoals21@outlook.com
